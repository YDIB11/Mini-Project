Architecture diagram (textual reference for rebuilding in PowerPoint using native shapes)

Box-and-arrow layout, left to right. All three encoders are frozen; only the
fusion module and the linear head are trained.

                                          [frozen]
        Video MP4 ───┬──> 1 fps frames ──> CLIP-ViT-B/32   ──> T_v x 512   ┐
                     │                                                      │
                     ├──> 16 kHz mono   ──> wav2vec2-base   ──> 64 x 768    ├──> [CLS] + modality embeddings
                     │     audio                                            │     │
                     │                                                      │     v
                     └──> faster-whisper ──> RoBERTa-base   ──> T_t x 768   ┘   2-layer Transformer

                                                                                  heads   = 4
                                                                                  GELU, pre-norm

                                                                            (2 logits)
                                                                                  │
                                                                                  v

Suggested PowerPoint reconstruction

1. Three left-side rounded rectangles, one per modality input, stacked vertically.
   Color them a neutral gray to signal "input data".

2. Three middle rounded rectangles for the frozen encoders, one per modality, aligned
   with their inputs. Add a small lock or snowflake icon to each, or write "frozen" in the
   top-right corner of the box.

3. Three thin arrows from each encoder into a single wider rectangle labelled
   "Fusion Transformer (2 layers, d=256, 4 heads)". Mark this box with a colored
   border to signal "the only trainable part".

4. One arrow out of the fusion box into a smaller rectangle labelled "Linear head".

5. Final arrow into a pill labelled "hate / non-hate".

6. Add tensor-shape annotations on each arrow leaving the encoders:
     vision: (T_v, 512)
     audio:  (64, 768)
     text:   (T_t, 768)

7. Add a small footer line under the diagram: "Only the fusion module and the linear
   head are trained; all three encoders are kept frozen for a fair modality ablation."

Color scheme suggestion

- Inputs:           light gray (#E5E7EB)
- Frozen encoders:  light blue (#DBEAFE)  with a lock/snowflake icon
- Fusion module:    accent color (e.g. EPFL red #FF0000 outline, white fill)
- Output pill:      green for non-hate side, red for hate side, or a single
                    dark-gray pill labeled "binary classifier"
